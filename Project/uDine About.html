Mission Statement

uDine strives to streamline the college dining experience through the use of online ordering.

About Us

uDine is a firm based in Boston, Massachusetts. Founded in May 2018 by two Wentworth Institute of Technology students, uDine specializes in online ordering for college and university dining halls. With uDine, you can place orders online which will improve order accuracy and give you more time to socialize with friends and colleagues or prepare for an upcoming class.

Additional Information

uDine shares its name with an Italian city (Udine).
